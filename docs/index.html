Platform Capabilities

Built for the Floor. Trusted in the Boardroom.

Six capability pillars, engineered so that operators move faster, managers see everything, and auditors leave satisfied.

01

Zero-Friction Work Capture

Built to get out of the worker’s way — fast, precise, and highly practical.

  • 10-Second Navigation: A streamlined mobile-first interface gets operators to the right project step and inputting data in 10 seconds or less.
  • Universal Work Templates: Track more than welding. Define custom work types — fit-up, cutting, assembly — save them as organization-wide templates, and capture any floor activity without slowing the team down.
  • Pragmatic Parameter Tracking: Logs custom parameters and dates instantly, without bottlenecking the floor with digital signatures for routine entries.
02

One Project, Two Live Views

The tree and the flowchart are two windows onto the same project — switch whenever you want.

  • Switch Views at Will: Every project is editable as a Tree or a Flowchart, toggled with one click. Edits in one view appear instantly in the other — no import, export, or conversion step.
  • Tree View: A clear, left-indented project tree for detailed and one-off structures.
  • Flowchart View: A visual editor for standardized, highly repeatable project structures.
  • One-Paste BOM Import: Copy your BOM straight out of Tekla or your CAD package and paste it into an Excel-like grid. WeldTrack auto-detects columns, maps every field, and builds your project structure in seconds. An afternoon of data entry becomes a single paste.
03

Management & Access Control

Top-down control that stays clean as your operation grows.

  • Anti-Bloat Manager Interface: The management app automatically hides any feature or data a user lacks permission to see — every screen stays clean and free of administrative clutter.
  • Live Project Editing: Modify in-progress projects by adding nodes or deleting empty ones. The system automatically locks and protects any node that already contains captured work.
  • Global Custom Roles: Create fully custom roles and manage access top-down, eliminating project-by-project permission headaches.
  • One-Click Document Generator: Pulls captured node data straight into your company’s paperwork templates for immediate printing — reusable across one project or the entire organization.
  • Role-Sensitive Notifications: An in-app dashboard filters critical warnings — expirations, maintenance — strictly by role, keeping leaders informed without spamming phones.
04

Qualifications & Procedures

Deep, automatic tracking of the credentials that keep you compliant.

  • Qualification Engine: Deep tracking for welder and non-welder qualifications. Automatically logs 6-month continuity from actual floor entries and warns before expirations.
  • End-to-End WPS/PQR Workflow: Define a preliminary WPS in the app, capture actual PQR parameters on the shop floor, import real values back, adjust ranges, and export a finalized WPS — with official files uploaded or linked at any stage.
  • Live WPQ Integration: Capture Welder Performance Qualification parameters on the floor and see them reflected in the management app instantly.
  • Fast Heat Propagation: Heat numbers are captured, linked to their parent MTRs, and automatically propagated down the project tree — full traceability without database bloat.
  • Strict Admin-Controlled Versioning: Every document, parameter, and consumable is versioned the moment it is captured. Records are locked against direct editing — strict compliance with no administrative bottleneck.
05

Floor Asset & Consumable Control

Proactive management of the tools and materials that keep the floor running.

  • In-App Maintenance Hub: Four urgency tiers. Workers raise maintenance requests from the floor app; maintenance personnel manage the whole workflow from the management app.
  • Universal Machine Tracking: Certifications and maintenance schedules for any machine — not just welding gear — with attached certification documents and automated renewal alerts.
  • Consumable Batch Tracking: Granular inventory logging for storage and reheat rules, with customizable low-stock alerts.
  • On-Demand Offline Access: Workers save crucial WPS or construction plans with one click for instant access anywhere on the floor — even in total Wi-Fi dead zones.
06

Bulletproof Data Sovereignty

Enterprise-grade security without the enterprise IT headaches.

  • On-Premise Appliance: Runs on a dedicated, containerized local server — fully insulated from forced OS updates and external cloud outages.
  • Absolute Data Ownership: Data stays on your physical hardware. Cancel anytime and retain permanent view-only access to all historical data for audits.
  • Optional Remote Access Module: Reach the management app or capture work from home, over mobile data, or in the field — as a small monthly add-on.
  • Post-Quantum-Safe Encryption: All internal network routing and the remote access module are secured with Post-Quantum Safe Encryption (PQSE).
  • Automated Local Backups: Customizable, frequent backups to isolated physical storage guarantee business continuity.

Powered by FacGuard

Zero-Trust Protection for Your IP

WeldTrack’s uncompromising local performance is protected by FacGuard, our zero-trust network overlay built on OpenZiti. FacGuard abstracts the network layer entirely: your factory infrastructure becomes invisible to external threat actors while remaining seamlessly accessible to authorized personnel — with full multitenant isolation between facilities and post-quantum-safe encryption end to end.

  • The Invisible Factory: Services are unreachable from the public internet — no open ports, no attack surface, no lateral network movement, no corporate espionage.
  • Secure Remote Oversight: Monitor operations and conduct audits from home or abroad through a post-quantum-safe encrypted tunnel.
  • Multitenant by Design: Each facility operates in its own cryptographically isolated tenant — strict separation, centrally managed.
  • Absolute Data Sovereignty: Your manufacturing data never touches a shared cloud server. The physical hardware remains entirely under your jurisdiction.
